How it works

Three steps. Start to finish.

We mean it when we say no setup. Open a browser. Go.

Snap a photo

Point the camera at their breadboard. The AI reads the parts — which little computer they have, what's connected, what's possible.

Describe what they want

"Make the LED blink when I press the button." Plain English. The AI writes complete, commented code — every line explained.

Watch it come alive

One tap sends the code to the board. It starts running. Change a number, re-send, watch the robot adapt. That's the whole loop.
